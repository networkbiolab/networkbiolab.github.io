About the lab

Our lab is focused in the development of computational methods to represent biological data as networks, its analysis and automatic generation of knowledge from the analysis of these networks.

We are focusd into several research topics, the most importants are determining genetic regulation in different conditions through the integration of ommic data, functional anotation of biological secuence and the representation of biological data as networks. To carry out those research topics, we use machine learning, clustering and optimization algorithms, also with the use of several tools in order to do protocols for the generation of biological netwoks in an automatic fashion.
